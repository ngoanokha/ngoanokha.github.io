We are Anokha

Anokha is a non-government organisation in Vellore, India directly benefitting more than thousands of children through various live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment.


Doubtless, education is the most powerful catalyst for social transformation. But child education cannot be done in isolation. Anokha aims at providing education through fun and innovative educational activities and bringing an inception of change in the society.

Events

Scientastic

To trigger the curiosity among the budding scientists, Anokha NGO conducts Scientastic every year. Students from various schools in and around Vellore, of various age groups, come to our campus, and we teach them Science in a fun and a creative way. We help the children build various projects and models. 'Scientastic 3.0' was held on 22nd February, 2020. Scientastic 3.0 basically involved an ideathon conducted for ingenious students from various schools in and around Vellore.

Awareness Initiative

Individually we all are drops, together we are an ocean. Anokha believes in involving thousands to bring a change in million realities. We organise various campaigns inviting the support of people around us to create an unforgettable impact on society. Many campaigns like sanitary napkin drive, notebook distribution drive, blood donation camp have been organised by Anokha.
